		  Let's Learn the Basics.
	     -----------------------------------------
1.Click on a letter button to try and guess if that letter is contained within the randomly chosen word.

2.If the letter is in the mystery word it will be displayed in its correct position on the line of dashes that represent each letter of the mystery word.

3.You start with 10 lives/chances and each time your chosen letter does notappear in the word you lose a life ,the Buttons are disabled and the graphics will show a man gradually being hung to represent this.

4.If the Letter are Present in the Word , the Button pressed is disabled.

5.If you have not completed the word within 10 attempts you lose the game and the man would be hung !

6.If you win with least possible attempts , your are awareded with a score of +10 for each remaing lives/chances.

7.You may change the player by : Option -> Player Change ; in the dropdownlist.
 
8.You may change the Game type by :Option -> Change Words; in the dropdownlist.
